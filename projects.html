Health Tracking & Goal Management Web Application

Full-Stack • Health Informatics • Data Analytics • Human-Centered Design

Vue.js Flask SQLite CSV APIs SMART on FHIR

Overview: Built a web application to help users track and accomplish weekly health and lifestyle goals through data-informed recommendations and motivational feedback.

Problem: With the growing availability of health-tracking technologies (e.g., Fitbit, Apple Health), users can collect lots of wellness data but often struggle to translate it into sustainable habits.

Solution: A goal-management platform where users set three weekly goals and receive quantitative feedback based on progress plus population-level analytics.

What I worked on:

  • Built the frontend in Vue.js for an interactive goal-setting and progress-tracking experience
  • Implemented backend services to support goal tracking, progress logging, and analytics
  • Stored user data in a SQLite database and recorded progress to CSV for analysis
  • Integrated positive reinforcement using a quote API (motivational feedback)
  • Integrated SMART on FHIR to connect with clinical health records (e.g., medical history)

MSUFCU Predictive Chatbot Experience (Fran)

Senior Capstone • Web App • Conversational UX • Data-Informed Recommendations

HTML CSS JavaScript MySQL Boost.AI Low-Code AI

Overview: Built a predictive financial-assistant chatbot experience for MSU Federal Credit Union (MSUFCU), enhancing their existing “Fran” chatbot to provide proactive, personalized guidance.

Problem: Members can manage accounts digitally, but often lack tools that help them anticipate low balances, avoid overdraft fees, and build better spending habits.

Solution: A web-based chatbot experience that analyzes account patterns to deliver supportive recommendations (e.g., suggesting bill payments after payday when balances are low), plus goal tracking and spending breakdowns.

Key features:

  • Predictive alerts based on observed low balance points and recurring bill timing
  • Recommendations to schedule payments strategically to reduce overdraft risk
  • Monthly spending goal setting and progress tracking
  • Category-based spending breakdown summaries

Tech: Web app built with HTML/CSS/JavaScript, integrated with Boost.AI chat panel and a remotely hosted MySQL database; chatbot logic trained/configured in Boost.AI’s low-code AI platform.
